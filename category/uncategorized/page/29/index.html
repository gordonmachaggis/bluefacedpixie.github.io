The Parable of Paul and Dick

…But Paul was not satisfied with his Seattle Experience. There was one last taste sensation…

It was the Sunday after Thanksgiving and Cindy and Paul had an early morning flight out from Seattle on Monday. They had done well – Thanksgiving dinner Thursday, Kwanjai and soiree at Rosemarie’s on Friday, kickass pot-luck on Saturday (and Mt. Si hike) and a nice little pub dinner at Bick’s that very evening.

But Paul was not satisfied with his Seattle Experience. There was one last taste sensation:

Paul needed a Dick’s adventure. (Not to be be confused with Bick’s).

This is fast food at it’s purest. “One extremely greasy thing and some other burger thing please, ” (I’m sure Paul can provide the details).

“Oh yes, and a fries for Gordon.” It’s only a $1 more after all.

Happiness.

Oh yeah, _and_ we took them to the airport next day. Who says Seattle isn’t the place to be.
