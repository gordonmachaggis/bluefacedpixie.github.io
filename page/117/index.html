5″ of snow

I was lucky this weekend. My friend Jim called and invited us to go snowboarding. Danika couldn’t make it (off learning new sports) but I roped Morgan into joining us and we headed to Crystal yesterday. They had received 5″ of spring snow and it was pretty damn fine. Certainly better than gardening. We spent an extra-ordinary amount of fun time in the Snorting Elk Bowl and also scooting about with Jim and Fidelma.

It’d rounded out a rather sporadic activity week nicely – I’ve had to change my work route due to the closed Elliot Bay Park, so lost a mile per day on the bike as the new route is more direct and less scenic.

Mon (3rd): nada (flying)
Tue: bike to work and have flat tire on way home
Wed: bike to/from work (coming home on Dexter)
Thu: bike to/from work
Fri: nada
Sat: nada
Sun: snowboarding at Crystal
