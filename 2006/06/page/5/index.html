Nice Burger

I don’t see why Danika should get to review all the food, especially as she wasn’t there ðŸ™‚

Post-Tiger last night we went to the Issaquah Brewpub – which I never knew existed in old town Issaquah. The Brewpub has a few of it’s own beers but has also been bought by the Rogue chain, which ensures a larger supply of micro-brews and a glossy standard of service and environment (I don’t know what it was like before).

I had a rather stellar Kobe Beefburger with blue cheese and (shock!) real fries! (no more Hilltop tavern with no deep fat fryer etc!).

Soft, tender, very full of flavour – a winner!

The beers were pretty good too. Adrian swears by the chocolate stout, and I tried a Chipotle Ale (nice but a little lighter than I was looking for) and an IPA-like beer I missed the name of (I think it’s the brewed-on-location beer) but which was very refreshing. [This sentence is too long to pass the Danika test, but I did use parenthesis.]

Best thing about biking is the calorie-intake afterwards.

Ride: Zipping Tiger

Another blast around Tiger tonight – this time with Adrian.

Legs were a little tired after Fiore runs this weekend but managed to climb the fire-road in 29 1/2 minutes, and overall we zipped about for 1:18 rolling.

Best conditions and riding overall since the first time out this year: dry weather with a little wet on the trails, some good mud and nice non-dusty dry trails. A few scares (there seem to be more drops every week) but everything in control.

Rudy’s with orange again.
